History

"Established in 2025. Riding the White Water Rapids Rafting Adventure was founded by Spring Schipper with a vision to give people the wild experience of exploring nature around them. Families have made history riding these wild white rapids. They enjoy the scenery around them as they float down the river"

We hope that you and your families enjoy your time spent on the river and ride the rapids like a champion. This will be a life changing experience in the making. We hope that you will come and vite us agian in the futrue.
